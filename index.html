common Mistakes made by Solo Female Travellers

If you are taking your first solo trip, there are a few mistakes you can make that will ruin your trip

Travelling solo differs quite a bit from travelling in a group, so it's easy to make mistakes when you travel for the first time. To help you prepare for your trip, here are some common mistakes women make when traveliing alone:

  • Not researching the local culture
  • Let your family know your travel plans
  • Be prepared for an emergency
  • Flashing expensive items
  • Partying too hard
  • Looking lost
  • Disclosing personal information
  • Ignoring maps
  • Not budgeting well
  • Not trusting your gut
  • Overpacking
